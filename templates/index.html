It's a win-win

Pikup aims to reshape the delivery industries by providing pickup and delivery services for any scenario with an auction pricing infrastructure. Pikup is a user-friendly app that allows customers to order any type of pick-up and delivery services, even for trips that don't require the user’s physical presence. The platform also allows drivers to bid for trips thus giving the drivers more control over their wages while also optimizing the price for the customer. It’s a win-win!
